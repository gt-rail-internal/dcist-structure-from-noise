Instructions:
This HIT requires you to complete a robot task where you need to find flags to collect points. There will be two phases, a tutorial mode and a final mode. This task will take you to another web page and is expected to take around 15 minutes. Please read the instructions below carefully before attempting it.


1. Start
Copy your Worker ID before you begin.

When you go to the external webpage, you will be greeted by this as below. Zoom out your browser window (usually ctrl -) if needed to see the whole thing.



Click on the 'GO’' button, you will be asked for your Worker ID in a dialog box.


After entering your ID, click OK.
**Note if you go back to this page when the dialog box is open, your browser will crash. Refresh the page and try again.




Task Objective:
Your task will be to find randomly appearing and disappearing flags around the map. They will become visible and give you points when your robots that you control are nearby.


Controls:
You will be able to select robots by click and dragging across them. Robots can be controlled whenever they are “connected” either directly or indirectly to the pentagon orange base on the top right. Disconnected agents can’t be controlled.



Below the instruction box are five buttons (with associated keyboard shortcut letters) which will enable you to control the selected robots. If needed, an additional action click will be asked for the control. The button’s functionality are as below:
Set Heading (H): Robots will face at the same angle and move in the same direction as your additional action click.

Go To (G): Robots will move towards the target you select with your additional action click.

Repel From (F): Robots will move away from the target you select with your additional action click.

Random (R): Robots will move randomly.

Stop (S): Robots will stop.




The tutorial task will end after 150 points or a few minutes and the main task will end after 2500 points or about 15 minutes. You are required to complete the tasks in a single sitting, and will not be allowed to re-attempt it. Do not refresh your web page before completing it. The webpage will remind you of these instructions and tell you when to return here.
